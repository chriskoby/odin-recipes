Easy Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick, nutritious meal bursting with colorful vegetables in a savory-sweet sauce. This versatile dish comes together in minutes, making it perfect for busy weeknights while still delivering fresh flavors and satisfying crunch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds
  3. Add vegetables, stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until crisp-tender
  4. Mix soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, water, and cornstarch in a small bowl
  5. Pour sauce over vegetables and stir until thickened, about 1 minute
  6. Serve hot over rice or noodles
